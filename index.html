What is MyFont?

MyFont is the ambitious idea and app that serves two purposes, first it leverages Deep learning to recognize your handwriting and converts your handwriting into a PC-usable font. This app is an application of offline handwriting recognition using Deep neural nets. The app will allow the user to click a photo of the handwritten content which should contain all the alphabets, digits present in english language(0-9, A-Z, a-z). The user can upload the image through their PCs too. The photo will then be passed in form of any of the many popular image formats to the Cloud. The pipeline including all the algorithms of character segmentation plus character recognition runs in the Cloud and each of the characters are recognized and the handwriting is recognized. For the font production part, these characters are converted into the font that you can download and use with your favorite word processor or office suite.
